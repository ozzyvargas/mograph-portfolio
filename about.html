About Me

Attentive to detail, adaptable, and a natural problem solver with creativity in my blood. Hi, I’m Ozzy.

I’ve been immersed in the arts from a young age from assisting my dad with wedding and quinceañera photography and videography, playing trumpet in a mariachi, and dancing competitively for eight years. Today, that creative foundation continues to flow in my blood through motion graphics, where I see everyday moments as frames, angles, and transition shots waiting to happen.

After nearly a decade in emergency medical services, I chose to return to my creative roots; bringing with me discipline, adaptability, and a drive to push the limits of what I can create through videos and web development.
