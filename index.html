About Me

Hi! I'm Ozzy. A self taught motion designer who rediscovered his creative side 3 years ago (shout out YouTube University!). It all started when my best friend gave me a Keyframes 101 crash course in the gym parking lot which then lead to my first job as a creator. I enjoy making videos that make the viewer ask "How did they do that?!" and the funny thing is, that's exactly how I learn. When I see something dope and want to deconstruct it so I can recreate it with my twist or just have the knowledge in case I could use it at a later time. Most of the things I create start as crazy ideas or inspiration I got, have no idea how to do it besides diving in and figuring it out.

P.S. I'm also a self taught web developer of about two months and built this website myself.
